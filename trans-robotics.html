Trans-robotics


Invited by Department of Cultural Affairs of Taipei, Y2K ( Youyang HU, Chiao-Chi Chou ) showcased their “Biosignal_Syn-thesasia” in Taipei Digital Arts Festival "Trans Robotics" exhibition from November 22, 2018 in Taipei.


The exhibition "Trans Robotics" touched on the topics of perceiving human beings to enhance their own perceived expansion through collaborative machines, and aims to connect artificial intelligence. This time, domestic artists are invited to discuss how different perceptions can reach the possibility of expansion. The six senses are "vision, hearing, touch, "Smell, taste, mind" is a classification. Through the cross-border cooperation between artists, industry and academia, to explore the real power or disability of people when they are super organisms? Can humans coexist with machinery or lead to mutual exclusion?
