Human Limitations – Limited Humanity


Invited by ARS ELECTRONICA, Y2K ( Youyang HU, Chiao-Chi Chou ) showcased their “Biosignal_Cybernation” in "Human Limitations - Limited Humanity" exhibition - one of the main theme in Ars Electronica Festival - "Out of the Box" from September 5 - 9, 2019 in Linz.


The exhibition "Human Limitations - Limited Humanity" revolves around the relationship between humanity and the environment, and our limitations therein. What socio-ethical obligations arise from our present technologies and our ever-increasing interaction with nature?

The future of humanity may be limited to the finite resources of the planet, but throughout history we have been able to stretch the limitations of our environment through agriculture and the domestication of plants and animals. The question is, are we able to use new technologies to calculate sustainable solutions to ensure we can continue to coexist with our natural world?
